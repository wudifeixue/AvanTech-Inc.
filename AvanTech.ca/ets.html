WebDRIL ETS provides oilwell drilling and well servicing companies with a valuable bridge between field operations and the office information system. Combining a powerful forms-based reporting system and the award-winning Microsoft SQL Server database, ETS provides professional reporting and improved availability of data, resulting in improved service to management, administration and operating sectors. ETS' integration with Payroll and Invoicing reports allows customers to avoid time-consuming data re-entry. ETS has optional modules such as HSE, which manages Health, Safety & Environmental information between the field and the office. ETS also instantaneously provides operating information such as repair hours, stand-by hours, rig move hours, rig-up hours and down-time hours.
