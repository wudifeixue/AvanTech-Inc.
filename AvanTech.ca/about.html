ABOUT


Our Mission: Bringing efficiency through intelligent automation

AvanTech Inc. has combined knowledge of what Advanced Measurements and FormSoft Software Solutions have created over the past 15 years with industry's newest technologies to create the ultimate enterprise management software package for the Drilling and Well Servicing industry.


AvanTech provides organizational data management solutions for oilfield service companies in the form of advanced software applications. Software solutions include equipment management, electronic tour sheets and HSE applications. AvanTech offers an industry proven, out of the box solution that meets the vast majority of the client requirements in its standard form.


AvanTech is allied with Pleasant Solutions, an Edmonton based software developer and integration company. AvanTech also retains close connections with Advanced Measurements, Inc.


Our vision:

Avantech's product and services improves our customers existing business processes through intelligent and innovative technology solutions.


Our core values:

  • Excellence: We are committed to unparalleled customer service, timely delivery and precision in what we do. We respect our customers above all else and never forget that they come to us by choice.
  • Teamwork: We treat one another with respect and communicate openly. We foster collaboration while maintaining individual accountability.
  • Community: We strive to help improve our communities in which we work and live. We believe that being socially responsible means giving back to the community of our time and resources.
  • Professionalism: We show pride, enthusiasm, professionalism and dedication in everything that we do, and we do it with honesty, integrity and humility.

    A quality approach to R&D:

    AvanTech follows a research and development plan that evolves with the changing market. The features and functionality of our software provides customers with profitable benefits. Over the last three years, our R&D spending has consistently stood at approximately 30% of revenue.


    Superior customer support:

    AvanTech offers superior customer support through JIRA requests, email, and a phone line that is staffed during regular office hours.
